Plant Care

UX Case Study

This project delivered an advanced solution for individuals with limited plant knowledge, comprising a self-watering 'globe' using vacuum technology for precise hydration, a soil moisture detector for timely alerts, and a QR code-linked online resource offering comprehensive plant care guidance.

Learning management system

UX Case Study

NTNU needs a new Learning Management System (LMS) to replace Blackboard. The project aims to design an efficient solution for students, instructors, and coordinators, employing user-centered principles. The goal is an adaptable LMS with a clear interface for various universities and colleges.

Mustad Festival

Visual branding

This project involved creating a comprehensive visual branding strategy for a dynamic festival, including eye-catching posters, distinctive drink tickets, and captivating graphic elements. The posters captured the event's essence, while the drink tickets seamlessly fit the theme. Diverse graphic elements adorned materials, enhancing the attendee experience and overall festival success.

RS Driftsservice AS

Visual branding

This project crafted a unified visual brand for "RS Driftsservice AS," focusing on property and unmanned petrol station maintenance in Namsos, Norway. I designed a logo, curated graphic elements, and created targeted marketing posts for the local community, effectively conveying the company's distinct services and values.
